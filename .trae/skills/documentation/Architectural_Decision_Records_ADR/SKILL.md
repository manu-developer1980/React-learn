# Architectural Decision Records (ADR)

## Overview
ADRs document significant architectural decisions, context, and consequences.

## Format
- **Title**: Short description.
- **Status**: Proposed, Accepted, Deprecated.
- **Context**: The problem being solved and constraints.
- **Decision**: The chosen solution.
- **Consequences**: Positive and negative implications.

## Where to store
- `/docs/adr/0001-record-architecture.md`
